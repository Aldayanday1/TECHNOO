featured places

Raja Ampat, Papua Barat

Raja Ampat is an amazing underwater paradise. Spectacular coral reefs, rich marine biodiversity and beautiful beaches make it the perfect place for divers.

Kepulauan Gili, Nusa Tenggara Barat

The Gili Islands are a tropical paradise located near Lombok, Indonesia. Consisting of three beautiful islands, namely Gili Trawangan, Gili Air, and Gili Meno.

Labuan Bajo, Manggarai Barat

Labuan Bajo is a stunning tourist destination on the Island of Flores, Indonesia. Famous as the gateway to the Komodo Islands, Labuan Bajo offers incredible natural beauty

Kepulauan Derawan, Kalimantan Timur

The Derawan Islands Consisting of several islands, including Derawan Island itself, this destination offers breathtaking natural beauty, pristine beaches, and remarkable marine life.

Gunung Berapi, Jawa Tengah

Mount Merapi is a captivating destination that offers an exhilarating experience for adventurous travelers. Known as one of the most active volcanoes in the world

Taman Nasional Komodo, NTT

Komodo National Park mesmerizes with its Komodo dragons, stunning beauty, and rich biodiversity. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, it's a must-visit for wildlife enthusiasts.
